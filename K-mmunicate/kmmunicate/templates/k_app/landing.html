What is K-mmunicate?

K-mmunicate is a web-based application for Filipino Sign language recognition. The name of the system is a combination of two words, "K" meaning "kinesics" (body movements and gestures regarded as a form of nonverbal communication) and "mmunicate" that derives from the word "communicate". The purpose of the system is to help people without hearing disabilities or speech-impaired people understand how FSL works.

What is the purpose of K-mmunicate?

With the help of the system, it will provide immediate assistance and support to anyone who seek a guide in interpreting Filipino Sign Languages. The system will benefit both people with and without speech or hearing disabilities. It will help people without knowledge of FSL to understand the language and at the same time, it will help people with hearing disabilities communicate easier, as people will now understand what they wanted to say. The system will serve as a guide to help both people with and without hearing disabilities communicate with one another, in an easier and effortless way.

K-mmunicate aims to assist users in identifying and understanding real-time Filipino Sign Language hand signals and seeks to support users by translating Filipino Sign Language into an audible speech.
